About

With a sharp eye for detail and a knack for creative problem-solving, I bring a unique perspective to my work. I have extensive experience in content writing as well as in graphic, web/visual design, UX/UI, branding, advertising and marketing.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration from the Hebrew University, with solid practical knowledge in graphic design and proficiency in softwares such as Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, and Figma. Two years ago, I also completed studies in UX/UI interface design.

Collaborative, quick to learn, and motivated, seeking to work with like-minded people on multidisciplinary projects.

Professional Background

I founded a boutique travel agency in Tokyo, where I lived for over 10 years. During this time I published "TOKYO IS YOURS" — a travel guide featuring 20 off-the-beaten-path tours — and created the associated website, blog, digital campaigns and PR.

Currently working in digital marketing with e-commerce brands across cosmetics, fashion, health, and lifestyle sectors. Previously worked as a project manager in humanitarian aid, financial trading software, and trading companies — with expertise in operational management, budget planning, partnerships, fundraising, and customer management.
